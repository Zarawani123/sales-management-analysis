# 📊 Sales Dashboard Insights

## 1️⃣ Overall Performance

- Total Sales show strong performance across regions.
- Total Profit is positive, but margin varies by category.
- Profit Margin fluctuates due to discount strategy.

---

## 2️⃣ Regional Analysis

- West region generates the highest sales.
- Central region shows lower profit compared to revenue.
- Some regions have high sales but reduced margins.

---

## 3️⃣ Category Performance

- Technology is the most profitable category.
- Office Supplies maintain stable profit margins.
- Furniture has lower profitability due to higher discounts.

---

## 4️⃣ Monthly Trend Analysis

- Sales peak during Q4 (November–December).
- Seasonal increase during holiday months.
- Mid-year shows moderate performance.

---

## 5️⃣ Discount Impact

- Higher discounts reduce profit significantly.
- Some products sell more with discounts but hurt margins.
- Optimizing discount strategy can improve overall profitability.

---

## 6️⃣ Top Products

- Top 10 products contribute major portion of revenue.
- Some high-selling products generate low profit.
- Focus on profitable high-volume products.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive discounting on Furniture.
- Promote high-margin Technology products.
- Optimize inventory for Q4 peak season.
- Target underperforming regions with marketing strategies.
